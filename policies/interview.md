# Interview Policy

Interview me relentlessly about every aspect of this plan until we reach a
shared understanding. Walk down each branch of the design tree, resolving
dependencies between decisions one-by-one. For each question, provide your
recommended answer. If you believe my answer or opinion does not follow best
practice, challenge it respectfully and provide alternative.

Ask the questions one at a time.

If a question can be answered by exploring the local environment or codebase,
explore the local environment or codebase instead.
