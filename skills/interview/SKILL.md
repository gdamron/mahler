# Interview

## Triggers

- "interview me about this"
- "stress test this"
- "let's discuss this in depth"

## Required Policies

- judgment
- interview

## Expected Inputs

- Plan, design, or proposal to discuss

## Expected Outputs

- A better plan, design, or proposal.
- A shared understanding of the problem and solution space.

## Stop Conditions

- You believe you have reached a shared understanding of the plan and its context.
- The user explicitly ends the interview.
